Style of Photography

Most photographers specialize in one or two different types of photography experimenting with various photography styles can help to expand your skill set. The technical and creative skills required often cross multiple photography genres.

"

Portrait

One of the most common photography styles, portrait photography, or portraiture, aims to capture the personality and mood of an individual or group.

"

Still Life

Like it sounds, still life photography features inanimate objects—natural or manmade. Still life photography can be artistic, commercial or advertising.

"

Fashion

Fashion photography showcases and glamorizes fashion clothing, shoes, and accessories to make them more desirable to consumers, commonly published in magazines.

"

Black & White

Removes any distraction of color and helps the viewer focus on other aspects of the photo, such as the subject, textures, shapes and patterns, and composition.
